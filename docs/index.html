What is JCOIN?

JCOIN is the utility token of the Join Ads Network, a programmatic advertising company founded in 2019 and operating across Latin America.

🎯

Publisher Rewards

JCOIN is used within the Join Ads Network to distribute rewards to publishers and content creators who participate in the advertising ecosystem.

🤝

Partner Incentives

Partners and affiliates in the Join Ads Network can receive JCOIN as incentives for contributing to the growth and efficiency of the platform.

⚡

Built on BNB Smart Chain

Deployed on BNB Smart Chain (BSC) for low transaction fees and fast confirmation times, making it practical for frequent micro-transactions.

Tokenomics

JCOIN features controlled emission with a hard cap on total supply and monthly minting limits to ensure sustainable token distribution.

Emission Mechanism

  • ✦ No pre-mine — Tokens are minted gradually as needed
  • ✦ Monthly cap enforced on-chain — Maximum 500,000 JCOIN per calendar month
  • ✦ Role-based minting — Only authorized addresses with MINTER_ROLE can mint
  • ✦ Burnable — Token holders can permanently burn their tokens
  • ✦ Pausable — Emergency pause mechanism for security incidents
