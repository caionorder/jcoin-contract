1. Overview

JOINADS COIN (JCOIN) is a BEP-20 utility token deployed on BNB Smart Chain (BSC). It is designed to serve as the reward and incentive mechanism within the Join Ads Network ecosystem, a programmatic advertising platform operating in Latin America.

JCOIN enables the Join Ads Network to distribute rewards to publishers and partners in a transparent, auditable, and programmable manner using blockchain technology. The token is not intended as a speculative asset or investment instrument.

2. Join Ads Network

Join Ads Network is a programmatic advertising company founded in 2019. The company operates in the digital advertising space across Latin America, connecting advertisers with publishers through its programmatic platform.

The company's core services include programmatic media buying, publisher monetization, and advertising technology solutions. JCOIN was created to complement these services by introducing a token-based reward system for the network's participants.

3. Token Utility

JCOIN is designed to serve specific functions within the Join Ads Network ecosystem:

Publisher Rewards

Publishers who generate advertising revenue through the Join Ads Network can receive JCOIN as additional rewards. This mechanism aims to incentivize content creation and platform participation.

Partner Incentives

Partners and affiliates who contribute to the network's growth and operational efficiency can receive JCOIN as incentive payments.

Ecosystem Participation

As the Join Ads Network ecosystem evolves, JCOIN may be used for additional utility functions within the platform, such as accessing services or participating in governance decisions.

Note: JCOIN's utility is limited to the Join Ads Network ecosystem. It does not represent ownership, profit-sharing rights, or claims on company assets.

Emission Model

JCOIN employs a controlled emission model with two key constraints enforced at the smart contract level:

  • Hard Cap: The total supply can never exceed 100,000,000 JCOIN. This limit is immutable and enforced by the contract.
  • Monthly Cap: A maximum of 500,000 JCOIN can be minted per calendar month. The cap resets automatically at the beginning of each month.

There was no pre-mine or initial token generation event. All tokens are minted gradually as needed for ecosystem operations, subject to the monthly and total supply caps.

Token Distribution

Tokens are distributed through the Join Ads Network platform based on publisher performance and partner contributions. Distribution is managed by authorized addresses holding the MINTER_ROLE.

6. Security & Access Control

The JCOIN smart contract implements a role-based access control system using OpenZeppelin's AccessControl library. This ensures that sensitive operations can only be performed by authorized addresses.

DEFAULT_ADMIN_ROLECan grant and revoke all other roles. Held by the contract deployer.
ADMIN_ROLECan pause/unpause token transfers and configure transfer restrictions.
MINTER_ROLECan mint new tokens, subject to monthly and total supply caps.

Safety Mechanisms

  • Pausable: All token transfers can be paused in case of security incidents or emergencies.
  • Transfer Restrictions: Optional mint/burn-only mode restricts transfers to minting and burning operations only.
  • On-chain Caps: Both monthly emission and total supply caps are enforced at the contract level and cannot be bypassed.
  • Open Source: The full contract source code is publicly available and verified on BSCScan.
